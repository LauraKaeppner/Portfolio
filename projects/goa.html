Gods Of Akragas

"Gods Of Akragas" is a game prototype developed by students of Augsburg University of Applied Sciences in collaboration with the Department of Classical Archaeology at Augsburg University. It is set in ancient Agrigento in the time around 500 BC and the design as well as elements of the story are based on historical findings.
