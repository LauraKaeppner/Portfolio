Beyond Any Gravity

Beyond Any Gravity is about Evens, an engineer who participates as a test subject in a research project on local gravity changes on the space station Daedalus. During the first test of the Gravity Glove, a severe anomaly occurs that transports Evens to a parallel world of the space station. With the help of the Gravity Glove, he must now find a way out of the anomaly that will otherwise corrupt him.

In the role of the protagonist Evens, the player explores the abandoned space station Daedalus to find new paths and information that will bring him closer to solving his way out.

Winner of the "Gamespreis 2022" in the category "Prototyp"
