Little Critters

Little Critters takes you back to your childhood!

Revel in the crisp autumn air and gather chestnuts. Let your imagination soar! Collect treasures from the woods and bring to life your own charming chestnut critters!

Click here to play the game. :)

Little Critters was developed during the 3-day Cozy Fall Jam 2024 hosted by Virtual Turtle Games.
